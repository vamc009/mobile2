Emergencies

If you smell natural gas, or see downed power lines, leave the area immediately and then call BGE at 800.685.0123

If your power is out, please call 877.778.2222

If a power line is down:

  • Do not touch or go near the downed power line.
  • Keep others away.

If you smell a gas leak:

  • Extinguish all open flames.
  • Do not use matches or lighters, and do not attempt to light your appliances.
  • Do not touch electric switches, thermostats, or appliance controls.
  • If the odor is strong, do not use your corded or wireless phone. All of these devices can cause sparks.
  • Leave the premises and go to a safe place.
  • Call BGE:800.685.0123 or 800.735.2258 TTY/TDD (Maryland Relay). This number is available for emergencies 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
  • Remain at the safe location and wait for BGE to arrive.
  • When reporting a power outage, your call goes directly to our trouble processing center for response. The system works by matching your phone number to your account, so it's important that we have your current phone number on file.
